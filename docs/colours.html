Colours

Colors in this design system are grouped into primary, secondary, and neutral to keep designs consistent and easy to understand. The primary color is used for main actions and important elements, like key buttons or links. The secondary color supports the primary and is used for less important actions or accents. Neutral colors are used for backgrounds, text, and layout elements to create balance and readability. To use these, you can copy and paste the relevant color classes into your HTML and apply them where needed. As long as you include the design system stylesheet, the colors will automatically be applied and display correctly without any extra setup.
