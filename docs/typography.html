Typography

Typography is used to style text in a clear and consistent way across a design, including headings, paragraphs, and labels. It helps improve readability and creates a clear visual hierarchy so users can easily understand the content. In this design system, typography styles are predefined for different text types, such as headings and body text. To use them, you can copy and paste the provided HTML or apply the relevant typography classes to your text elements.
