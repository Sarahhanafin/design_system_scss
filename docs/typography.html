Typography

The typography system uses clear hierarchy and simple styles to improve readability. Heading 1, Heading 2, and Heading 3 are used for titles and section headings, while body large and body small are used for main text and supporting content. Labels help identify buttons and form fields, and captions are used for small notes or extra information.
